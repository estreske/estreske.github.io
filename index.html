About

Welcome to the personal website of Eric Streske! I am an NYC-based, full-stack web developer, originally from Chicago, IL. I have a Bachelor's degree in Guitar Performance and spent several years touring nationally with the Shoeless Revolution, a jam/rock band from St. Paul, MN. I have had several jobs in the software industry including support, implementation, training, and project management. In December 2013, I enrolled in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive, a 12 week boot camp for full-stack web development.

I also enjoy travelling, singing, building PCs, bowling, computer gaming, and comedy television. My wife and I were married on June 26th, 2013 and currently live in the Astoria neighborhood of Queens, NY.
